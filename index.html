Profile Summary

Dr. Dogga Raveendhra is working as an Assistant Professor (Grade-I) in Electrical Engineering Department at NIT Warangal and Prior to he served as an Assistant Professor (Grade-II) Electrical Engineering Department, MNNIT Allahabad, Prayagraj for a period about 3 Years. Before joining MNNIT he was with ALTEN GmbH, Germany as a Senior Engineering Consultant. Dr. Dogga completed his Post-Doctoral Fellowship Programme at Renewable Energy Research Center, AUS, UAE along with the affiliation of Senior Research Engineer at Zunik Energies Pvt. Ltd. followed by Ph.D. from Electrical Engineering Department, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee with Excellence in Doctoral Research Award from IIT Roorkee for best Ph. D. Work. Dr. Raveendhra Dogga, Also Founder of Zunik Energies Pvt. Ltd., which is an incubated Start-up of IIT Roorkee, funded by TIDES Business Incubator, IIT Roorkee, accelerated and supported by AP Innovation Society. During his Ph.D. has been awarded BASE Fellowship by DST and IUSSTF to pursue his research at an internationally renowned laboratory, FREEDM System Center, NC State University.

Recently, he has received two Government sponsored projects worth of 48 Lakhs from DST under Device Development Programme, and 15 Lakhs from MSME. In Over all, Dr. Dogga credited one granted patent and published two patent disclosures at NS State University, USA and two Indian patents, 18 highly reputed Journal publications in IEEE, Elsevier, IET and Energies, etc., including 5 Book chapters & 35+ IEEE International Conference Papers.

Raveendhra is credited various awards for his research, named for Albert Nelson Marquis Lifetime Achievement Award by Marquis Who's Who in 2017 and also his Name listed in Who's Who in the World 2019, 2018 and 2016 by Marquis (USA). He has been nominated by IIT Roorkee for Festival of Innovation and Entrepreneurship (FINE-2018), Rashtrapati Bhavan, invited as a delegate for CII DST India-Italy summit and FINTECH 2018. He has been selected by Invest India and AP Innovation Society as an International delegate from India for SLUSH 2018, which was held in Finland. His innovation has been awarded the IITRHF Judges choice award and also the 1st Prize in the IKMC 2018 Startup Exhibition as a Most Promising Innovation Award-2018. He Chaired a Session at 10th IEEE Conference Session (SCORed2017) in Malaysia. He has been invited for mentoring for start-ups for Troubleshoot Inc.' organized by Student Alumni Interaction Cell (SAIC), Thapar University, Punjab.

Raveendhra is currently serving as a Reviewer for more than 15 Journals of repute including IEEE Transactions, Elsevier, and IET Journals. Currently he is serving as an associate Editor for various International Journals of repute. He is serving as a technical program committee member for more than 30 International Journal, for government summits, technical program committee member for conferences which were held in abroad and also serving as a Reviewer for more than 25 IEEE International conferences which were held in abroad. He is having 10+ years’ experience from Academic, Industrial, and R&D in the field of Renewable and Power Electronics Applications.
